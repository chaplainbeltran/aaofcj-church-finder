About This Directory

AAOFCJ Church Finder — Independent Community Resource

Constitutional Basis
AAOFCJ Constitution (2025) — Article 2, Clause V
"To procure, by all possible means, mutual help that form the Apostolic Assembly, and to promote the growth and expansion of the work of our Lord Jesus Christ in the United States and in other countries of the world."
"Procurar, por todos los medios posibles, la ayuda mutua de todos los que forman la Asamblea Apostólica, y promover el crecimiento y la expansión de la obra de nuestro Señor Jesucristo en los Estados Unidos y en otros países del mundo."

A free, independent resource for anyone searching for an Apostolic Assembly congregation — members relocating, visitors traveling, or those finding their way to an Apostolic Church for the first time.

⚠ This is not an official AAOFCJ publication. Information is compiled from publicly available sources and is updated periodically. To report an error or missing church, use the Correction or Feedback options available in the app.
